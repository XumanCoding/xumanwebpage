Projects

Project Title: PyShorty - The Python-Powered URL Shortener

Objective: PyShorty is a sleek and user-friendly URL shortener built with Python. It aims to provide a quick and efficient way to shorten long URLs, making them easier to share and manage.

Technologies Used: Python, Flask, SQLite, hashlib

Challenges Faced: One of the main challenges was ensuring the uniqueness and security of the shortened URLs. Implementing a robust hashing algorithm was crucial to prevent collisions and maintain the integrity of the links.

Results: PyShorty successfully launched and has been serving users with an intuitive interface and reliable performance. It has shortened over 10,000 URLs to date, with a zero collision rate.
